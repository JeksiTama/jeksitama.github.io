Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 March 2025

Thank you for playing Pop n Solve ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

At this time, we do not collect any personal information directly from users.

AdMob (Google)

Our game uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data, such as:

  • Device information (e.g., device ID, model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interactions and viewing data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by AdMob is used to:

  • Show personalized and non-personalized ads
  • Analyze and improve ad performance

3. Third-Party Services

Our game includes third-party services like Google AdMob. These services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review AdMob's data usage in the AdMob Help Center.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have collected such information, please contact us, and we will delete it.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly for updates.
